How It Works

1

Choose Your Van Size:

Select the perfect van size for your items.

2

Specify Furniture Items:

List the items you need to move, such as boxes, sofas, tables, fridges, beds, and mattresses.

3

Select Date and Locations:

Pick your preferred date for the move and specify your pickup and delivery locations.

4

Confirm and Book:

Review your details and confirm your booking with a click.

5

Van Delivery:

Sit back and relax! Your selected van will arrive on time to safely transport your items to their destination.
